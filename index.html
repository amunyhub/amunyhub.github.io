The Pursuit of Happiness

As a part of or Project 1 project we were placed in small groups and asked to find, clean, and analyze a set of data.

The Wolrd Happiness Report is a Publication of UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network and is based on Gallup polling. The goal of the report is to use happiness indicators to inform policy making decisions. The data we analyzed looks at 170 countries spanning five year period from (2015 - 2019).
